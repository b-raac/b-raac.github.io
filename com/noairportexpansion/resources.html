Other websites

For more information on airport impacts, an excellent resource is Trenton Threatened Skies, a community advocacy group fighting the expansion of Trenton-Mercer Airport. There is no affiliation whatsoever between BRAAC and Trenton Threatened Skies. We just admire a website well done, and link to it as a good resource.

https://trentonthreatenedskies.com/resources/

Another excellent resource is Airport-Hunterdon.com, a website offering "Independent Solberg Master Plan 2024 Information." There are many current and historical documents available on this website. There is no affiliation whatsoever between BRAAC and Airport-Hunterdon.com. We just admire a website well done, and link to it as a good resource.
